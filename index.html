About Us

Welcome to German Guru Nepal!

We are a premier language center dedicated to teaching the German language to students in Nepal. Our experienced instructors and immersive curriculum ensure that you will master the language efficiently and effectively.

We believe in providing a holistic learning experience that not only focuses on language skills but also on cultural immersion. Our programs are designed to cater to all levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

Our Courses

We offer a range of courses tailored to meet the needs of different learners:

Beginner Course - A1

Ideal for those starting from scratch. Covers basic grammar, vocabulary, and conversation skills.

Elementary Course - A2

For learners who have a basic understanding of German and want to improve their skills.

Intermediate Course - B1

Designed for advanced learners focusing on fluency and advanced grammar.

Upper Intermediate Course - B2

Enhance your German proficiency with advanced grammar, enriched vocabulary, and fluent conversation skills.

Crash Course

Intensive and fast-paced German language training designed for rapid learning and quick proficiency.

Exam Preparation A1 - B2

Specific courses aimed at preparing for exams like the Goethe-Zertifikat and TestDaF.

Frequently Asked Questions

What levels of German do you teach? ▾

We offer courses for all levels, from beginner to advanced. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to improve your fluency, we have a course for you.

Do you offer online classes? ▾

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes to accommodate your needs. Our online classes are interactive and provide the same quality of education as our in-person classes.

How can I enroll in a course? ▾

You can enroll in a course by contacting us through our website, email, or phone. We will guide you through the enrollment process and help you choose the course that best fits your needs.

Why should I learn with German Guru Nepal? ▾

Our teaching methods and approach stand out from other language centers. We emphasize cultural immersion alongside thorough exam preparation. Students gain access to a wealth of learning materials. Additionally, German Guru Nepal provides personalized counseling for applying to Germany and assists with the visa process as well.

What is the duration of each course? ▾

The duration of each course varies. Generally, each level (A1, A2, B1, etc.) can be completed in 2-4 months depending on the intensity of the course. For more details click here.

How much does it cost to learn German? ▾

The cost varies depending on the level and type of course. Please check our pricing section for detailed information.
